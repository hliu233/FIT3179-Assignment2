How is Coronavirus affecting the world

This visulisation will demonstrates Coronavirus data from different aspects.

Coronavirus distribution globally

This map shows us an overview on how coronavirus case is distributed cumulatively among countries. It is not hard to observe that there is some darkest blue on the map which is an indication that these countries have a very high proportion of people got infected by coronavirus out of their total population.

An example would be the United States of America, out of its full population, there is nearly 2.3% of its people reported as coronavirus infected.

Cumulative case over time

The line chart on the left shows us how cumulative case changes over time among each country. Three countries have a very different trend that set them apart from the rest of the world which are the United State of America, Brazil and India respectively.

The graph shows us that as time goes by, the majority of the country is able to get the coronavirus situation under control slowly. Nevertheless, it is not the case for these three countries, there is an obvious trend that the cumulative case is not only increasing but also increasing at a relatively fast pace.

Male is facing a higher risk in Coronavirus situation

There are a lot of studies as well as researches been undertaken to try to find out the different copy-mechanism between gender when we are dealing with the current coronavirus situation. By viewing the diverging chart on the side, it shows us how the death case has been distributed between male and female in all the countries that have data collected.

We notice that for most countries, the Male group generally do present a much higher death proportion in the overall death cases within the country. So we have the reason to believe that Male is facing a higher risk in this coronavirus situation.

Coronavirus is more fatal for People over age 60

Using the collected data, we see on the left the death percentage among different age groups within these countries.

It is observed that within all the death case caused by Coronavirus, the proportion that been distributed in the age group of 1-60 is very few. By contrast, the proportion that been distributed in the group that has an age over 60 is huge. We notice the trend that the proportion gets bigger when the age increases, and its largest proportion is allocated to the highest age group of 90+.

Therefore, it is an indication that Coronavirus is more fetal for the group of people age over 60.
